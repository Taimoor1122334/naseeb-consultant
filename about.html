A Decade of Integrity

Empowering Careers Since 2011

Naseeb Consultant was established in 2011 with a simple vision: to bridge the gap between talented Pakistani students and prestigious universities in the UK, Turkey, China, Lithuania, Poland, France, Russia, and across Europe. Over the past 15 years, we have grown from a local Lahori counseling center into one of Pakistan's most trusted international education agencies.

We handle admissions portfolios for UK, Turkey, China, Lithuania, Poland, France, Russia, and other European destinations. Our counseling staff ensure that visa filings align perfectly with the latest embassy rules.
